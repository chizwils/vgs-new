Who we are

Vecchia Global Services is a firm that provides bespoke management strategy/implementation services to the Nigerian gaming industry and helps clients increase their customer base. We do this by;

• Providing technology ( apps/games/B2B software ) that increase customer acquisition and retention. We provide innovative general or custom made technology for our clients based on their needs/goals, and based on any recommended qualitative/quantitative VGS strategy on how they can grow their customer base.

• Providing smart strategy in profit increase, market share increase, marketing, new market entrance, competitive responses, and any specific goals our clients may have.

Declining Profitability Analysis and Solutions Implementation

We offer a top down approach to an operator's declining profitability problem. Why is your profit declining? What revenue stream is affected? Is it tied to a general gaming market behavior or trend? Are your operational costs increasing? Are you experiencing a declining volume of bets placed on your platform? Why? Or maybe there is no decline, but you want to increase your profits? A VGS analysis encompasses all aspects of the problem or goal.

We collate all relevant data, identify the possible causes, and propose long lasting solutions through qualitative and quantitative methods.

Market Share Increase

As an operator, an increase in market share more often than not leads to an increase in profit, and a growth of your brand. A big part of the Nigerian gaming industry is made up of the online market, as more and more punters are placing their bets and gaming online. How can you increase your online market share? How can you acquire and retain more punters? How can you position your brand as the go-to online betting platform for punters? VGS provides an in depth analysis of your problem or goals, and proffers solutions ( technological and/or strategic ) to help you increase your online market share.

Marketing

New Market Entrance Analysis

For new operators planning an entrance into the Nigerian gaming market, VGS provides an in depth analysis of the market; history, trends, and projections in order to help them properly understand the market and make the right decisions. We help you enter the market in the most optimal way, from branding, to marketing, to operational strategy, VGS is your one stop shop for an efficient market entrance.

Competitive Response

In an ever changing industry, operators have to constantly innovate, or react to changes from competition. VGS strategy will always put you at the forefront of innovation, and will guide a competitive response if the first move is made by the competition. Often times, these responses can be the difference between maintaining/increasing your customer base, or losing a part of it to the competition.

Financial Models/ Projects Economics Analysis

VGS offers a robust projects economics analysis over a projected time period. We build financial models by collating data and calculating key economic indicators like NPV, VIR, PB, UTC, etc in order to give our clients the cashflows, revenues, and profits on a project over a time period, discounted to the present value of money. We make projections with different sensitivities on vigorish, number of punters, daily betting/gaming behaviors, etc. We also analyze and compare different project options, and advise our clients on which is a more profitable investment option.
